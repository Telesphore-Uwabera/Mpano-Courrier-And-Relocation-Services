Relocation Expertise in Kigali and Provinces

Mpano Courier And Relocation Services Group specializes in relocating individuals and families within Kigali, including the districts of Kicukiro, Gasabo, and Nyarugenge, as well as to provinces across Rwanda.

We began our operations in 2024, offering personalized services tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients. Our commitment to seamless and efficient relocation processes has established us as a reliable partner in Rwanda's relocation sector.

Our foundation is built on exceptional customer service, dedication, and ongoing staff training, ensuring a reputation for outstanding service delivery.

Import and Export Services

Our experienced team handles all aspects of logistics, customs clearance, and freight management, ensuring a smooth import/export process to and from Rwanda.

We participate in industry-leading workshops and international conferences, keeping our expertise current and ensuring that we provide world-class service.

Our operations are underpinned by a strong ethical framework and legal compliance, giving our clients peace of mind when partnering with us.
