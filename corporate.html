Unparalleled Expertise in Rwandan Logistics, Infrastructure, and Customs

Mpano Courier And Relocation Services Group has been providing top-tier services for corporate, government, and diplomatic clients since 1993. This focus forms the backbone of our business, making us the preferred moving company for many of the largest organizations operating inRwanda.

We offer specialized services and facilities tailored to the unique needs of our corporate, government, and diplomatic clients. These services ensure a seamless and efficient relocation process, addressing the complexities and specific requirements of each client type.

Our Group is built on a foundation of customer service, hard work, dedication, and staff training. This commitment has driven our growth and earned us an excellent reputation for communication and quality service.

Unparalleled Expertise in importing and exporting to Rwanda from every location worldwide.
