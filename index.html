ABOUT US

At Mpano Courier and Relocation Services, we are more than just a courier and relocation company. We are a dedicated team that strives to build lasting relationships with the people and communities we serve. Our core mission is to make everyday tasks easier, whether it’s delivering packages on time, ensuring a smooth move, or creating personalized interior spaces that truly feel like home.

Established with a focus on reliability, trustworthiness, and sustainability, Mpano Courier and Relocation Services offers a comprehensive range of services designed to cater to both residential and commercial clients across Rwanda. Our services include same-day and next-day courier deliveries, complete relocation solutions, and customized interior design services, all aimed at providing our customers with peace of mind.

OUR LOCATIONS: Based in Kigali, Rwanda, we aim to establish a strong market presence not only within Kigali but also in other major cities across the country within our first year.

OUR FUTURE PLANS: We are committed to expanding into neighboring countries within five years, bringing our reliable and stress-free services to even more communities in the region.

Whether you need a trusted courier service, help with a move, or expert advice on transforming your living or working space, Mpano Courier and Relocation Services is here to make your life easier every step of the way.
