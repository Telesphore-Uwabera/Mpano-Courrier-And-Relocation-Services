THE RELOCATION PROCESS

There are many reasons for moving and each move is unique.

International relocations usually start with a survey and assessment in order to provide a quote or estimate for your move.

Dates are then set for our professional packing team to pack your belongings. Packing can take a number of days.

Each box will be carefully numbered and labelled. A packing list will be created which you will be asked to sign before the packages are loaded.

Your household goods will then be transported in a sealed container to the port if your goods are going by sea.

If your goods are going by air they will be taken to the airport.

Our operations manager will give you an estimated time of arrival in your new country home.

Your shipments will be tracked as it travels and your destination agent will keep you up to date.

You will need to ensure all the paperwork is in order to import your household goods into your new country. Our team will advise.

Once your goods arrive they will be offloaded and go through the processes of port and customs clearance.

The goods will then be released and can be taken to storage or delivered to your new address.

Our teams will carefully check your belongings against the original packing list and reposition and unpack them.

Debris can be removed on the day of unpacking and the team leader will ask you to sign to say your goods are received.
